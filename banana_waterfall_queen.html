Reclaiming Our Streets

Welcome to Our Community

Our streets are in need of reclaiming after a recent crime wave. We have assembled a group of volunteers to reclaim our community. Our goal is to increase safety and create a welcoming environment for everyone.

Together we can make a difference. Whether you want to participate in a community clean-up, or help organize a community event, we invite you to join us!

Volunteers

We are always looking for new volunteers to join our community. Every volunteer is important to us and we value the contribution they make. Whether you can volunteer once a week or once a month, we appreciate every effort.
